Our Story

Generations of
flavor, one kitchen.

We started Mezbaan with a single, stubborn belief: that Indian street food, done properly, is one of the greatest culinary traditions in the world. Not watered down. Not simplified. The real thing.

Every item on our menu is built from recipes that have been passed down, tested, and refined over generations. We source carefully, cook honestly, and serve with pride.

"The same warmth, the same crunch, the same explosion of flavor — brought straight to Saudi Arabia without compromise."
